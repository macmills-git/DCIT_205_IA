Meet Our Staff

John Daniel

Professor

Areas of Expertise: Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning

John Daniel is a professor in the Department of Computer Science. With an extensive background in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, he has made significant contributions to the field. His research focuses on developing advanced machine learning algorithms and applying them to solve real-world problems.

Jaden Smith

Associate Professor

Areas of Expertise: Database Systems, Data Science

Jaden Smith is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science. She specializes in database systems and data science. Her research interests include data mining, big data analytics, and database performance optimization. She is passionate about leveraging data to gain valuable insights and improve decision-making processes.

Mark Johnson

Assistant Professor

Areas of Expertise: Cybersecurity, Network Security

Mark Johnson is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science. His expertise lies in the field of cybersecurity and network security. He is dedicated to developing robust security measures to protect sensitive information from cyber threats. His research focuses on network intrusion detection, secure communication protocols, and vulnerability analysis.

Solomon Aggrey

Assistant Professor

Areas of Expertise: Human-Computer Interaction, User Experience

Sarah Lee is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science. Her research revolves around Human-Computer Interaction and User Experience. She explores ways to design intuitive and user-friendly interfaces, conducting user studies to understand human behavior and improve the overall user experience. She is passionate about creating technology that positively impacts people's lives.

Robert Cooper

Associate Professor

Areas of Expertise: Software Engineering, Agile Development

Robert Cooper is an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science. His research focuses on software engineering practices and Agile development methodologies. He aims to improve software quality, productivity, and collaboration through effective software engineering practices. His expertise lies in software architecture, software testing, and project management.
